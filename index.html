About Us

A Family Business Built on Quality

Brannack Electric Inc. has been serving the greater Hartford County area since 1980 — over four decades of trusted, family-owned electrical service. Founded by Timothy J. Brannack, we're not a franchise or a call center. When you call us, you get us.

With President John Brannack leading operations and 424+ completed permitted projects across Connecticut, we've built our reputation one satisfied customer at a time. BBB A+ rated. Top 4% in CT on BuildZoom. Nextdoor Neighborhood Favorite.
