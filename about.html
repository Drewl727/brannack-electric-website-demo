Our History

Bloomfield's Trusted Electrician for Over 45 Years

Brannack Electric Inc. was founded on May 1, 1980 by Timothy J. Brannack with a simple mission: deliver honest, quality electrical work to the families and businesses of Bloomfield and Hartford County. More than four decades later, that mission hasn't changed.

What started as a small local operation has grown into one of Bloomfield's most recognized electrical contractors — with over 424 completed permitted projects across Connecticut and a reputation built one satisfied customer at a time. Today, President John Brannack leads the company with the same values and hands-on commitment that Timothy established.

We're not a franchise or a national chain. When you call Brannack Electric, you speak with someone who knows your neighborhood, knows the local inspectors, and will stand behind every job we do.
