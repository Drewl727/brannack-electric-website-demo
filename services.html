Electric Inspection

A thorough electrical inspection is the foundation of a safe home or business. Our licensed inspectors examine your entire electrical system — panel, wiring, outlets, grounding, and more — to identify code violations, safety hazards, and potential failure points before they become expensive or dangerous problems.

Whether you're buying or selling a property, renovating, or simply want peace of mind, Brannack Electric provides comprehensive inspections that meet Connecticut state code requirements.

  • Full panel and breaker inspection
  • Outlet, switch, and GFCI/AFCI testing
  • Grounding and bonding verification
  • Wiring condition assessment
  • Code compliance review
  • Written report with findings

Electric Panel Installation

An outdated or undersized electrical panel is one of the most common causes of tripped breakers, flickering lights, and fire hazards. Brannack Electric specializes in full panel upgrades — from 100-amp to 200-amp or 400-amp service — for homes and commercial buildings throughout Hartford County.

With 424+ permitted projects completed, our team handles all permitting, inspection scheduling, and utility coordination so you don't have to.

  • 100A, 200A, and 400A panel upgrades
  • Main breaker and sub-panel installation
  • Permit filing and inspection coordination
  • Utility company liaison
  • Surge protection installation
  • Load calculation and capacity planning

Electric Panel Repair

Is your panel throwing breakers repeatedly, running hot, or making a buzzing sound? Don't ignore it — these are signs of a failing panel that can lead to electrical fires or complete power loss. Our licensed electricians diagnose and repair panel issues fast, minimizing downtime for your home or business.

  • Breaker replacement and testing
  • Burnt or damaged bus bar repair
  • Double-tapped breaker correction
  • Loose connection tightening
  • Federal Pacific and Zinsco panel evaluation
  • Grounding and bonding corrections
Schedule Panel Repair
